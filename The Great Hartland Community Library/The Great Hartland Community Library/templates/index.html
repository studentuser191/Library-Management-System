Welcome to the Great Hartland Community Library

Use the menu above to manage the library.

The Great Hartland Community Library, run by a dedicated team of volunteers, serves a close-knit community by providing access to a diverse collection of books. Currently, the library operates using a manual system, which, while effective, is inefficient and prone to errors. This system makes tasks such as book management, checkout, and record-keeping time-consuming and cumbersome.

To improve efficiency and enhance the service quality for its patrons, the library is looking to adopt a digital solution. The goal is to streamline daily operations, including keeping accurate records, managing checkouts, and conducting searches for books. With a computer-based system, the library will be able to provide a more seamless and user-friendly experience for its members. The long-term vision includes integrating a comprehensive digital platform to support all aspects of library management.
